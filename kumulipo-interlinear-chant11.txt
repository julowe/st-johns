## The Eleventh Era

Line 0708: Oia wahine noho lani a pi‘o lani no  
She was a woman living among chiefs and married to her brother  
She that lived in the heavens and Piolani (married her brother),  
Line 0709: Oia wahine haulani a noho lani no  
She was a restless woman living among chiefs  
She that was full of enjoyments and lived in the heavens,  
Line 0710: Noho no iluna a iho pi‘o ia Ki‘i  
She lived above and came bending down over Ki‘i  
Lived up there with Kii and became his wife,  
Line 0711: Weli ai ka honua i na keiki  
The earth swarmed with her offspring  
Brought increase to the world.  
Line 0712: Hanau o Kamaha‘ina, he kane  
Born was Kamaha‘ina [First-born], a male  
Kamahaina was born a man,  
Line 0713: Hanau o Kamamule, kona muli  
Born was Kamamule, her younger born  
Kamamule his brother;  
Line 0714: Hanau o Kamamainau, o kona waena  
Born was Kamamainau, her middle one  
Kamainau was born next,  
Line 0715: Hanau o Kamakulua kona poki‘i, he wahine  
Born was Kamakulua her little one, a girl  
Kamakulua was born, the youngest a woman;  
Line 0716: Noho Kamaha‘ina he kane ia Hali‘a  
Kamaha‘ina lived as husband to Hali[‘a]  
Kamahaina lived with Hali.
